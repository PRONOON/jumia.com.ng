HOW TO USE THE SEARCH INTERFACE

  1. Select your criteria in any or all of the boxes and the search results grid will fetch profiles that match your selection to the top of the results.

  2. Profiles that match 4 of your WANTS will be listed first before profiles that match 3 are. And then 2, 1, and 0.

  3. Any result that doesn't match your LOCATION SELECTION will not be displayed. To get all results, keep it in ALL.

  4. Click on the corner profile info box to go to her main profile. You'll be redirected to a new browser tab/window to avoid refreshing your Search selection.
